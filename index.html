Welcome to Our New Home

Aavo, Padharo! — A new chapter begins!

We are thrilled to welcome you to our brand-new home in the beautiful Sanctuary at South Gate community in Indian Trail, North Carolina. After months of anticipation, we're finally ready to open our doors and share this special milestone with the people who mean the most to us — you!

Join us as we perform the sacred Griha Pravesh and Vastu Puja ceremony in the morning, seeking blessings for our new home in the traditional Gujarati Brahmin way. The puja will be followed by a delicious lunch with wonderful company. Come be a part of this auspicious occasion and help us fill this house with blessings, laughter, and love.
